About us

Aqours (pronounced as "aqua") is a school idol group that was originally formed and led by Kanan Matsuura two years prior to falling apart and was eventually succeeded by Chika Takami in Love Live! Sunshine!!. They were the school idol group of Uranohoshi Girls' High School. The original group consisted of three members (Third years). A new group led by Chika was founded after coming across the name (which is later revealed to be written by Dia) and started using it unofficially until the group was officially assembled when the third years joined. At the beginning of the movie, the group continues with six of the members at Numazu High School following their temporary integration at Seishin High School and the name is later used again by new members for their idol group at the end. They debuted with the song Kimi no Kokoro wa Kagayaiteru kai?.
